Services

We stand by our pedals and designs at Radio Cyclone. All pedals are eligible for free technical maintenance for a full year after purchase.

• • •

All pedals are available with custom paint and art by request. Traditional models are only made in quantities of 50 but tribute models (i.e., Fuzz Ewe II) are available.

• • •

We also offer paint and remodeling work of your existing, non-Radio Cyclone pedals.

• • •
