About

Radio Cyclone pedals are handmade by an Austin, TX small business. Each pedal is crafted to capture a precise feel perfect for a standout pedal and recognizable tone.

We make only 50 of each pedal design before discontinuing the line, ensuring not only the exclusivity of your pedal, but also fresh ideas from us at RC. Our PCBs and enclosures are supplied by BYOC and all extra parts are locally sourced. Our goal is give you a visual representation of the uniqueness of your sound.
